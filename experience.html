EXPERIENCE

May-October 2022

As soon as I finished my STPM examination, I worked as Sales Assistant at Big Pharmacy Raub while waiting for my STPM and University results. In this phase, I decided to work to fill my savings to pursue my studies later. Also, I can help my family even a little. From this work journey, I gained many experience and skills, especially how to communicate with customers. Furthermore, it adds my level of patience because I need to handle many customers such as old folks and others. Then, I get to know all kind of medicines and its functions. I love the environment of my workplace because all the seniors are very kind and they kindly taught me if there's anything I don't know. On the time I first worked there, there are 2 new workers as me. They don't discriminate us and just treat everyone equally.
